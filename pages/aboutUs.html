ABOUT US

Founded in April 2011, LeWa Mobile Limited is a mobile internet company located in Zhangjiang High-Tech Software Park (HQ), providing Android ROM with in-depth customization and Operating Platform with distributive services and contents as well.

LeWaOS creates more chances and enhances competitiveness for worldwide OEMs, moreover, supplies more smart equipment with connectivity capability and service. The achievement lies in the integration from underlying architecture, protocol layer, data and algorithm, privacy and security, multi-media, interaction, visual design to supply chain and localized services.

REVA Operating Platform serves brand vendors, manufactures and advertisers to attract and keep end users’ stickiness, maximize the profitability of mobile device industry by the means of supplying contents distribution and big data presentation, OS-level system security and control mechanism.

VISION

To make every mobile terminal supported by more pleasant internet service.
